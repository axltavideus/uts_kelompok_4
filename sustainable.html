Sustainable Fashion

Exploring fashion that cares for the planet and society.

What is Sustainable Fashion?

Sustainable fashion emphasizes environmentally and ethically conscious design throughout the garment life cycle, reducing harmful impacts on the planet and people.

The Life Cycle of a Garment

Understanding the life cycle of a garment helps in identifying the environmental, social, and ethical impacts at each stageâ€”design, production, use, and disposal.

  • Design: Choices in fabric, materials, and patterns can reduce waste and environmental impact.
  • Production: Ethical production practices ensure fair wages and working conditions.
  • Use: Designing durable garments minimizes waste by extending their life cycle.
  • End-of-Life: Garments can be reused, recycled, or upcycled to reduce waste in landfills.

Sustainable Design Strategies

Fashion designers can implement sustainable strategies such as zero-waste design, using eco-friendly materials, and designing for longevity.

  • Zero-Waste Design
  • Using Low-Impact Materials
  • Designing for Longevity
  • Closed-Loop Systems
